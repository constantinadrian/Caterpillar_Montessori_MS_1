Our Mission

Caterpillar Montessori open its doors for the first time in 2012 to help the community from Portmarnock and surrounding area. We are pleased to say that after 8 years we have become a large childcare that provide the best service a child need.

Over the years we have provide a safe, secure and stimulating learning environment, where children can learn and grow at their own pace.

Our Values

Some of the key elements of good practice to support the learning and development of a child at Caterpillar Montessori are:

  • Maria Montessori 5 principles:
    1. Respect for the Child.
    2. The Absorbent Mind.
    3. Sensitive Periods.
    4. The Prepared Environment.
    5. Auto education.
  • Our team which is selected with great care to meet our high standard of care. Each member of our team is high experience and have highly qualification that it will give you the peace of mind that your children are cared for with kindness and expertise.
  • Our healthy lifestyle of nutritional meals, daily exercise and educational play promotes a healthy life for each child individually.

Our Services

We offer services for children aged between 2 years and 6 months - 5 years. Here at Caterpillar Montessori we provide 3 type of sessions:

  1. Morning session 09:00 – 12:30.
  2. Afternoon session 12:45 – 16:15.
  3. Full day session 09:00 – 16:15.

HSE approved, Fully insured and we take part in the ECCE scheme.

ECCE Scheme

Caterpillar Montessori is a participant in the ECCE (Early Childhood Care & Education) scheme.

Find more about the ECCE scheme.

We offer high quality Montessori curriculum, a session of 3 hours in the morning or afternoon, 5 days a week, 38 weeks a year.

There are flexible and optional additional services available that can suit your needs.
